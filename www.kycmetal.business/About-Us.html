About Us

As a leading graphite producer and supplier in the carbon industry in Malaysia, KYC Metal Industries Co., Ltd. has expanded its business to offer that quality globally. Our product serves as a vital component in the electric arc furnace (EAF) steel-making process. We manufacture and supply high quality carbon and graphite products to the global market in a wide range of industries, including the iron and steel, aluminum, automotive, machinery, metallurgy, and electronics industries.


We have a state-of-the-art production infrastructure that incorporates the latest technology for mixing, forming, baking, impregnation, graphitization, machining, and shaping, ensuring that our products are of the highest quality and adhere to the most rigorous standards. And we have a world-class team of graphite experts with decades of experience in production, application, and research. Our team is dedicated to providing tailored solutions and unparalleled technical support to help our customers achieve their objectives. 


Quality is as always our key focus. We maintain a rigorous quality control system to ensure that every product we sell meets or exceeds our customers’ expectations, and use the most advanced testing equipment and techniques to guarantee the quality and performance of our products. 


we continue to innovate and deliver specialized products that meet the evolving needs of our global clients.
